Executive Summary

TalentAI B.V. operates an AI-powered candidate screening and ranking system. Based on our assessment, this system is classified as a High-Risk AI System under EU AI Act Annex III, Section 4(a) — employment, workers management and access to self-employment.

Of the 8 mandatory obligations, 2 are critical gaps, 3 are partially addressed, 1 is compliant, and 2 have not been started. Immediate action is required on Risk Management and Human Oversight obligations before the August 2026 deadline.

Obligation Assessment
1. Risk Management System — CRITICAL GAP
No documented ongoing risk management process identified. EU AI Act Article 9 requires a continuous risk management system covering the entire lifecycle of the AI system. No evidence of this in uploaded documentation or public information.
→ EU AI Act Article 9 | EUR-Lex Regulation 2024/1689
2. Data Governance — PARTIAL
Data governance policy exists (v2.1 uploaded) but does not address training data representativeness or bias testing as required by Article 10. Dataset documentation is incomplete.
→ EU AI Act Article 10 | EUR-Lex Regulation 2024/1689
6. Human Oversight — CRITICAL GAP
No human oversight mechanism documented. Article 14 requires that high-risk AI systems enable human monitors to understand, intervene, and override system decisions. Current product flow shows fully automated ranking with no override capability.
→ EU AI Act Article 14 | EUR-Lex Regulation 2024/1689
5. Transparency — COMPLIANT
Website and product clearly discloses AI-powered candidate screening to users. Instructions for use provided. This obligation is met.
→ EU AI Act Article 13 | EUR-Lex Regulation 2024/1689
Prioritised Remediation Roadmap
Priority Action Required Obligation Effort Deadline

P1 Create and document Risk Management System Risk Management 1-2 weeks April 2026
