## Logo

### Wordmark (primary logo)
```
LexSutra
```
- **"Lex"** — Gold (`#c9a84c`), Playfair Display, Bold
- **"Sutra"** — White (`#e8f4ff`), Playfair Display, Semi-bold
- No space between the two words — they run together as one

### In Canva
- Font: **Playfair Display** (available in Canva as a Google Font)
- Weight: Bold for "Lex", SemiBold for "Sutra"
- Set "Lex" text colour to `#c9a84c` and "Sutra" to `#ffffff` or `#e8f4ff`

### Logo variations
| Variant | Background | "Lex" colour | "Sutra" colour |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary (dark bg) | `#060a14` navy | `#c9a84c` gold | `#e8f4ff` white |
| Light (reports, print) | `#f4f0e8` cream | `#b8903a` darker gold | `#1a1a2e` dark navy |
| Monochrome | Any | Same colour | Same colour |

### What NOT to do
- Don't add an icon/symbol — the wordmark stands alone
- Don't use a drop shadow
- Don't stretch or distort
- Don't put on a busy background without sufficient contrast
- Don't use a different font — Playfair Display only

## Typography

### Fonts (both available free on Google Fonts and in Canva)

| Font | Role | Canva name |
|---|---|---|
| **Playfair Display** | Headings, logo, display text | `Playfair Display` |
| **DM Sans** | Body text, UI, labels | `DM Sans` |

### Usage rules
- **Headlines** → Playfair Display, Semi-bold or Bold
- **Sub-headings** → DM Sans, Semi-bold (600)
- **Body text** → DM Sans, Regular (400)
- **Labels / eyebrows** → DM Sans, Semi-bold, ALL CAPS, wide letter-spacing (0.1–0.14em)
- **Legal / small print** → DM Sans, Regular, 10–11px

## Visual Style

### Aesthetic
**Dark · Premium · Authoritative** — law firm meets deep tech.
Not cold or clinical. Not playful or startup-casual. Serious, but with warmth from the gold.

### Key visual principles
1. **Dark backgrounds always** — never white backgrounds on marketing materials (save cream/white for the PDF report only)
2. **Gold is precious** — use sparingly. It should feel like a highlight, not wallpaper.
3. **Generous whitespace** — the design breathes. Don't crowd elements.
4. **Subtle borders** — use `rgba(255,255,255,0.06)` or `rgba(201,168,75,0.15)` to define sections without hard lines
5. **No drop shadows** — elevation is communicated through background colour layering, not shadows
6. **Grain texture** — a very subtle noise overlay (2.5% opacity) on the main navy background gives depth. Replicate with a fine grain/noise overlay in Canva.

### Card style
- Background: `#0d1827` (Navy 800)
- Border: 1px solid `rgba(255,255,255,0.07)` or gold variant `rgba(201,168,75,0.2)`
- Border radius: 12–16px
- Padding: 24–32px

## Canva Quick-Start Checklist

1. **Add brand colours** → Brand Kit → add all hex codes above
2. **Add fonts** → Brand Kit → Playfair Display (heading) + DM Sans (body)
3. **Set background** → `#060a14` for dark designs, `#f4f0e8` for print/report
4. **Logo** → Text element: "Lex" in `#c9a84c` + "Sutra" in `#ffffff`, Playfair Display Bold, no gap
5. **Grain overlay** → Add a noise/grain element at 2–3% opacity over backgrounds
6. **Check contrast** → All body text must be readable against the navy backgrounds
